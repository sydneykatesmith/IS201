About

The pom style of dance is a high-energy, sharp, and precise dance style that incorporates the use of poms (the colorful pom-poms you often see cheerleaders use). It blends elements of jazz, hip hop, and cheerleading with a strong emphasis on clean lines, formations, and synchronized movement.


My Experience

I was on my high school's Varisty Pom team for my Junior and Senior Year. I loved the athleticism and difficulty of POM style dance. I loved being able to perform and cheer on my school at both basketball and football games.





Steps to Make the Team

  1. Prepare for the audition (weeks before)
    • Learn necessary tricks
    • Stretch consistently
    • Practice stamina
    • Try learning a POM dance to understand the style
    • Practice cheer technique

  2. Pack Your Bag
    • Jazz Shoes
    • Hip Hop Shoes
    • Practice Poms
    • Water
    • Outfit you can move in

  3. Show up on Time

  4. Give It Your All!
